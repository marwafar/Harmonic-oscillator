feynman_diagram_GB_NR: compute the non-rephasing ground state bleach of the feynman diagram.
feynman_diagram_SE_NR.py:compute the non-rephasing stimulated emission of the feynman diagram.
feynman_diagram_ESA_NR.py: compute the non-rephasing excited state absorption of the feynman diagram.

feynman_diagram_GB_R: compute the rephasing ground state bleach of the feynman diagram.
feynman_diagram_SE_R.py:compute the rephasing stimulated emission of the feynman diagram.
feynman_diagram_ESA_R.py: compute the rephasing excited state absorption of the feynman diagram.

fourier-transform-NR.py, fourier-transform-R.py: compute the fourier transform for the R and NR component.
pure_absorp.py: compute the sum of the R and NR.

slice-2D-peak.py: allows to analyze a give slice of the spectra. One needs to define w_1, w_3 regions of interest

plot-2D.gnuplot: plot the 2D spectra using contouring plot.
